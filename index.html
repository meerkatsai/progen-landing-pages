With ProGen, you can typically start seeing weight loss as early as the first 3 days, with more noticeable results within the first few weeks. The program is designed to promote steady and sustainable fat loss while preserving muscle mass, ensuring long-term success. Remember, consistency is crucial in achieving your goals!
With ProGen, you're less likely to feel weak or tired while losing weight. The program is carefully designed to ensure you receive all the necessary nutrients, especially protein, to keep your energy levels up and maintain muscle mass as you lose fat. Additionally, ProGen provides specially crafted multivitamins and electrolytes to ensure you stay well-nourished and avoid fatigue. While your body may need a short adjustment period at the beginning, any feelings of fatigue are usually temporary. Following the program closely, staying hydrated, and getting enough rest will help you stay energized throughout your weight loss journey.

If you follow the ProGen program's guidelines and maintain healthy habits after completing it, you can minimize the risk of regaining weight. The program includes:

Post-Diet Chart: A detailed plan provided by the dietitian for ongoing eating habits.
Lifestyle Counseling: Ongoing advice to help you adopt sustainable habits.
Genetic Testing: Analysis of genes related to weight gain to tailor your diet based on genetic insights.

Adhering to these recommendations is crucial, as weight management relies on caloric balance. If the guidelines are not followed, weight gain is possible.

For the best results and overall well-being, combining the ProGen diet with regular exercise is recommended as part of a balanced lifestyle.
