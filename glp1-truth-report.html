01

GLP-1 is not the miracle social media shows

Clinical trials consistently show 0.5–1 kg per month — the same rate as a standard diet. The dramatic videos are shot at month 12–18 with best-case patients. Not averages.

02

The early weight loss is mostly water, not fat

GLP-1 depletes glycogen stores. Glycogen holds 3–4× its weight in water. The dramatic first-week drop? Mostly fluid. It returns when the drug stops.

03

You lose muscle, not just fat

Research shows 25–40% of weight lost on GLP-1 is lean mass. Losing muscle slows metabolism permanently and makes weight regain almost inevitable. VLCKD protocols preserve 95%+ lean mass.

04

When you stop — the weight returns fast

BMJ 2026 (9,000+ patients): average regain of 0.4–0.8 kg per month after stopping. Full return to baseline weight within 1.5–1.7 years.

05

Your health improvements also reverse

Blood pressure, cholesterol, and blood sugar gains disappear within ~1.4 years of stopping treatment. The benefit was real — but fully contingent on never stopping.

06

It does not fix why you gained weight

GLP-1 suppresses appetite. It does not address root causes: metabolic dysfunction, hormonal imbalance, insulin resistance, or eating behaviour. Stop the drug, the patterns return.

07

It could cost ₹15,000–25,000 every month — forever

Since stopping means regaining, patients seeking permanent results face lifetime monthly injections at ₹1.8L–₹3L per year. A one-time supervised programme costs a fraction of that — with permanent results.
