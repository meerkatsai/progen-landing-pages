The Science Behind It

How nutritional ketosis resets your metabolism

When carbohydrate intake drops below the body's threshold, it shifts its primary fuel source from glucose to fat — a state called nutritional ketosis. The liver converts fat into ketone bodies, which become the brain and muscle's preferred fuel. This isn't starvation. It's a deliberate metabolic switch that burns fat directly, spares muscle tissue, and dramatically improves insulin sensitivity. Clinical studies confirm the shift happens within 3–5 days and stabilises within 2 weeks, at which point fat oxidation increases by up to 11% and remains elevated throughout the programme.
